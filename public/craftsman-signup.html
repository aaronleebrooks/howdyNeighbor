Welcome to Howdy Neighbor!

A few ground rules for Craftmen

  • There are no stupid questions! This is a place for amateurs to communicate with professionals. Feel free to inform DIY-er's how to talk about your craft, but do not riticule them.
  • This is a unique platform to show your stuff! The DIY-er's are here to learn from your experience, not hire you for a job. Feel free to connect to potential clients, but your first priority should be to help!
  • Please be kind and helpful to all members of our community. Please do not swear, flame, or show any disrespect.
